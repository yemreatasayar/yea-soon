Who is

I'm a designer and art director with over a decade of experience across agencies, in-house teams and freelance collaborations. I work at the intersection of strategy, culture and creativity, building visual identities, campaigns and digital experiences with a strong focus on communication.

My background in copywriting, storytelling and central role in long-form design processes helps me shape ideas before turning them into visuals. My style leans toward clarity, bold color decisions and disciplined, minimal compositions.

Recently, I've been exploring the intersection of design and technology through tools such as BatchFlow and miniapps, focusing on creative automation and AI-assisted development.

Currently based in Berlin, I work remotely while pursuing a master's degree.
